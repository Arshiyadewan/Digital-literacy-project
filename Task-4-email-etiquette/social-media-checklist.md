# Social Media Do’s

1. Enable Privacy Settings: Regularly review and update your account settings to control who can view your personal information and posts.

2. Think Before You Post: Always consider the long-term impact of your content, as your digital footprint can remain permanent.

3. Maintain Professionalism: Use appropriate profile pictures and ensure your posts reflect a positive and professional image.

4. Verify Information Before Sharing: Check the authenticity of news, messages, or links before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.

5. Use Social Media Productively: Follow educational pages, connect with professionals, and engage in meaningful discussions related to your field.


# Social Media Don’ts

1. Do Not Share Sensitive Information: Avoid posting personal details such as your address, phone number, or live location.

2. Do Not Engage in Negative Behavior: Stay away from cyberbullying, online arguments, or posting harmful comments.

3. Do Not Post in Anger: Avoid sharing emotional or impulsive content that may harm your reputation.

4. Do Not Tag Without Consent: Always take permission before tagging others in posts, photos, or videos.

5. Do Not Click on Suspicious Links: Be cautious of unknown or misleading links, as they may lead to phishing or security threats.
